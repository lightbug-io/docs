# Frequently Asked Questions

This FAQ focuses on the Lightbug platform, services and technology.

You'll find a more general FAQ on the [support site](https://support.lightbug.cloud/support/solutions).

## Can the trackers send infomation to our own servers?

Yes, we provide many options for this:

- [APIs](/platform/apis): You can use our APIs to pull data from our servers to yours.
- [Webhooks](/apps/cloud/account/notifications#api-push): You can configure webhooks to send data to from our servers, to your servers.
- [Silos](/silos): You can run your own instance of the Lightbug platform on your own servers.
