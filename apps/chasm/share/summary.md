# Summary

## Overview

The first section of the summary includes graphs showing the overall and per message type message rates.

![](https://upload.r2.lb.chasm.cloud/2026/03/chrome_0Lb5bpk6HX.png)

## Statistics

There is then also a breakdown of the various message types, when the first and last message of that type appears in the share, a total count and overall average frequency.

![](https://upload.r2.lb.chasm.cloud/2026/03/chrome_i40urnq6cI.png)

## Raw Messages

At the very bottom of the page, all messages are displayed in their JSON format, which is a human readable translation of the raw binary data sent by the device (V3 Messages).

![](https://upload.r2.lb.chasm.cloud/2025/10/chrome_8vEOMkBnUk.png)
