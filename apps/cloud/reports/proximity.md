# Proximity Report

The proximity report shows other devices and BLE tags that were near the selected device during a given time period.

It can be filtered to show or hide unknown devices (devices that are not in your account) and BLE tags.

![](https://upload.r2.lb.chasm.cloud/2025/10/imgur/emE8bjE.png)

The report works both for devices that do BLE scanning, as well as for BLE devices that have been seen by multiple devices.
